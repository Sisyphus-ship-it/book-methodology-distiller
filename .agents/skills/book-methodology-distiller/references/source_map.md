## Status Rules

- `full`: source text has been inspected in this session or a cited local artifact was read.
- `partial`: table of contents, selected chapters, notes, or excerpts were inspected.
- `unread`: only bibliographic information or user-provided title is available.
- Mark public-knowledge inference explicitly in `Notes`.
- Never add page numbers, chapter claims, or exact source positions unless they were verified.
- Prefer 3-5 high-quality samples or passages over large sets of weak, noisy materials.
